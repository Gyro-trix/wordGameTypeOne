In this game the objective is to spell three letter words that are animals.


Here are the basics:

  • Three letter words that do not repeat a character
  • Overall you can only use a chacter once per session
  • Score is determined by how common the word is, time left when finished
  • Score is removed for using backspace or entering an invalid word

For example "Bee" would not work, the "e" repeats. Also if it was valid, "b" and "e" would not be usable going forward.
